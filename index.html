About Us

We thank our team of Singers, Instrumentalists, Sound Engineers, Video professionals and Management who have contributed for the achievement of our 68 Episodes reaching 5,15,000 Viewers and 3835 Subscribers on our channel within an year of inception.

We are now a strong team of 50 members since our inauguration and are growing after adding strength to the foundation by being inclusive of their advise and suggestions from our mentors. We have an unique record of broadcasting singers from ages eleven to seventy years. Our versatile covers of songs from 1959 ( Jayabheri) to 2019 ( Majili) a period of over seventy years can be experienced in our list. We have maintained the quality and the heart of the original compositions. We have invested for the growth of our channel only from the contributions and efforts of the Directors of the company . We are expecting some seed capital and crowd funding to develop and grow gradually.We are supported by an experienced team from Chennai namely SBS Digitek LLP for our digital marketing support.
We are proud to announce that we are a pioneer in this field to encourage artists of all age groups and help in promoting their talent globally .Our dedicated efforts and support from our members are carefully navigated to keep its value and ethics right. Our organisation ,though has its constraints, has developed into a community with guidelines and principles of social values to motivate the artists in them.
